# Style Library

Curated artistic styles for sketch-to-video variations. Organized by energy level for story arc planning.

## Grounded / Low Energy (good for openings and endings)

| Style | Prompt Keywords | Energy | Mood |
|-------|----------------|--------|------|
| **Professional Pencil** | detailed graphite pencil, cross-hatching, anatomical study, cream paper, fine art sketch | Low | Precise, academic |
| **Watercolor** | soft wet-on-wet watercolor, gentle color bleeds, visible paper texture, pigment granulation | Low | Gentle, painterly |
| **Ink Wash** | sumi-e ink wash, minimalist brushstrokes, zen aesthetic, rice paper, monochrome with grey gradients | Low | Meditative, elegant |
| **Charcoal** | expressive charcoal drawing, smudged shadows, dramatic contrast, rough textured paper | Low | Raw, emotional |
| **Blueprint** | technical blueprint style, white lines on navy blue, measurement annotations, engineering schematic | Low | Technical, precise |

## Warm / Medium Energy (good for early story beats)

| Style | Prompt Keywords | Energy | Mood |
|-------|----------------|--------|------|
| **Clean Cartoon** | clean vector cartoon, flat shading, bold outlines, children's book illustration, warm earth tones | Medium | Friendly, approachable |
| **Anime** | anime cel-shading, vibrant colors, dynamic pose, expressive eyes, Studio Ghibli aesthetic | Medium | Adventurous, warm |
| **Pixel Art** | 32-bit retro pixel art sprite, limited palette, SNES-era RPG aesthetic | Medium | Nostalgic, playful |
| **Storybook** | golden age children's book illustration, rich muted palette, narrative scene, visible brushwork | Medium | Whimsical, timeless |
| **Pop Art** | bold pop art, Ben-Day dots, primary colors, thick black outlines, Lichtenstein/Warhol inspired | Medium | Bold, energetic |

## Intense / High Energy (good for climax sections)

| Style | Prompt Keywords | Energy | Mood |
|-------|----------------|--------|------|
| **Psychedelic LSD** | vivid rainbow spectrum, liquid trails, melting edges, tie-dye bleeding, 1960s acid poster, op-art | High | Chaotic, euphoric |
| **Psychedelic DMT** | sacred geometry fractals, mandala patterns, jewel tones, Alex Grey inspired, cosmic hyperspace | High | Transcendent, awe |
| **Psychedelic Shroom** | bioluminescent, mycelium patterns, organic flowing forms, earthy with electric highlights, enchanted forest | High | Organic, mystical |
| **Neon Cyberpunk** | neon glow, rain-slicked streets, holographic UI, dark background, blade runner aesthetic | High | Futuristic, electric |
| **Glitch Art** | databent, corrupted pixels, RGB channel split, VHS distortion, scan lines, digital decay | High | Disruptive, edgy |

## Dark / Dramatic (good for tension and conflict)

| Style | Prompt Keywords | Energy | Mood |
|-------|----------------|--------|------|
| **Eldritch Horror** | Lovecraftian, tentacles, impossible geometry, dark writhing mass, cosmic dread, Junji Ito | High | Terrifying, alien |
| **Dark Gothic** | gothic cathedral, stained glass, deep shadows, candlelight, medieval manuscript illumination | Medium | Mysterious, sacred |
| **Film Noir** | high contrast black and white, dramatic shadows, venetian blind light, detective pulp style | Medium | Moody, cinematic |
| **Brutalist** | concrete textures, stark geometry, industrial decay, muted greys, oppressive scale | Low | Heavy, imposing |

## Ethereal / Dreamlike (good for transitions and bridges)

| Style | Prompt Keywords | Energy | Mood |
|-------|----------------|--------|------|
| **Oil Painting** | rich impasto oil painting, visible palette knife strokes, classical composition, gallery lighting | Medium | Luxurious, timeless |
| **Stained Glass** | luminous stained glass, lead lines, jewel-toned translucent panels, cathedral light | Medium | Sacred, radiant |
| **Ukiyo-e** | Japanese woodblock print, flat perspective, wave patterns, cherry blossom, Hokusai style | Medium | Serene, structured |
| **Art Nouveau** | flowing organic lines, Mucha-inspired, decorative borders, muted pastels with gold accents | Medium | Elegant, flowing |
| **Vaporwave** | pastel pink and cyan, Greek busts, retro 80s graphics, sunset gradients, glitch overlays | Medium | Nostalgic, dreamy |

## Suggested Story Arcs

### "The Awakening" (grounded → intense → grounded)
Pencil → Watercolor → Anime → Cartoon → Shroom → LSD → DMT → Pixel → Cartoon → Watercolor

### "Into the Void" (light → dark → light)
Storybook → Anime → Pop Art → Neon Cyberpunk → Eldritch → Glitch → Pixel → Cartoon → Watercolor

### "Evolution of Art" (historical progression)
Charcoal → Ink Wash → Ukiyo-e → Oil Painting → Art Nouveau → Pop Art → Pixel → Glitch → Vaporwave

### "The Trip" (reality → psychedelia → recovery)
Pencil → Anime → Shroom → LSD → DMT → Eldritch → Pixel → Cartoon → Watercolor
